About US

We believe in nurturing writers through guidance, recognition, and community.

The Simple Script Writers’ Project, an annual writing competition, is a writing project that was founded in 2024 primarily aimed at supporting writers in Ghana and in the near future all writers globally. To be seen, acknowledged and rewarded for the talents and abilities they possess. It's an opportunity for those who love to scribble out their thoughts and those who find solace in reading. Not all stories would be published, but truly, all stories can be seen.

Our Mission

To provide a transparent and accessible platform that supports, showcases, and rewards writers by sharing competition information, celebrating outstanding stories, and offering guidance that nurtures creative expression and literary growth.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized writing platform where every writer is seen, acknowledged, and empowered, fostering a vibrant community of storytellers and readers who find purpose, solace, and opportunity in the written word.

Our Journey So Far.

First Edition Winners

The Simple Script Writers’ Project started its first edition in 2024 at Breman Asikuma Senior High School, the founder’s alma mater. In the first edition, the theme was “School/Family Life” and we received 20 submissions from the students, and the winners Nhyiraba Kwasi Baah, Gifty Owusuaa Saka, and Trennice Thompson received 2000 Ghana cedis, 1000 Ghana Cedis and 500 cedis Ghana Cedis respectively. The first winner, Nhyiraba Kwasi Baah also received a scholarship which seeks to support him through his university education. We also gave books (non-fiction) to all the students who sent in their submissions.

Second Edition in 2025

In the second edition in 2025, the team decided to expand the competition into the mainstream media, since our main goal is not just in senior high schools but for all writers in Ghana to participate. So we made it into two, one for high school students and one for adults. We received 15 submissions in all, some from high school students and a few from adults who had seen it on social media. Our theme for the 2025 Adult Edition was ‘Ghanaian Historical Fiction’, capped at a maximum of 5000 words, which sought to see how writers would be able to learn, research and write their stories on the wings of the many histories that has held the nation up to this moment. The theme for the high school students remained “School life”. The cash prizes for 2025 are 7000 Ghana Cedis, 4000 Ghana Cedis and 2000 Ghana cedis respectively for the first, second and third winners for adults, and the prizes for the senior high schools are 3000 Ghana Cedis, 1500 Ghana cedis and 1000 Ghana Cedis for the high school students, which will be announced and given on 27th January, 2024. The winners are only three ultimate winners in total, and their cash awards depend on their position and on whether they are high school students or adults. For every year, the theme will change, in order to give writers the chance to explore other themes with their writing.
